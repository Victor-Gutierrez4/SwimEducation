Advanced Workouts

High-intensity drills designed for competitive swimmers or those ready to take their training to the next level. Focus on endurance, distance, and technique refinement.

Swim 3x800 meters freestyle with 1-minute rest between each. Focus on consistent pace, controlled breathing, and efficient turns. Track your time and try to reduce your split each round.

Use hand paddles for 5x100 meter freestyle sets. Keep elbows high and focus on pulling through the water with full arm extension. Build upper-body strength and reinforce proper stroke mechanics.

Put on swim fins and complete 8x50 meter sprints at max effort with 20 seconds rest in between. This enhances your leg drive, kicking strength, and explosiveness in races.

Swim a 200 meter freestyle, broken into 4x50s with 10 seconds rest between each 50. Focus on keeping your pace even or descending (getting faster each round). Repeat 3 times.

Using a kickboard, perform 25m, 50m, 75m, 100m, then back down (75m, 50m, 25m) of flutter kicks. Focus on tight core, small fast kicks, and consistent breathing. Great for building leg endurance.
