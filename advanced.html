Advanced Workouts

Challenging drills and high-intensity training for competitive swimmers

Training Techniques

For swimmers looking to push themselves further, the following drills focus on endurance, power, and race preparation. These techniques are designed to elevate your swimming to a competitive level.

  • Endurance Sets: 3x800m freestyle with minimal rest. Focus on even pacing and steady breathing.
  • Paddle Drills: Use hand paddles for 5x100m sets to build upper body strength and improve pull mechanics.
  • Kickboard Pyramid: 25m, 50m, 75m, 100m, then back down. Focus on fast, tight flutter kicks.
  • Broken 200s: Swim 4x50m with 10 seconds rest between each. Practice consistent or descending pacing.
  • Sprint Intervals with Fins: 8x50m at full effort. Rest 20 seconds between each. Improves explosiveness and kick strength.

Tips for Advanced Swimmers

  • Track your times to measure improvement and adjust intensity.
  • Use training equipment like paddles, fins, and snorkels to isolate and enhance different skills.
  • Incorporate dryland workouts to strengthen core and overall conditioning.
  • Prioritize recovery with stretching, hydration, and rest days.
