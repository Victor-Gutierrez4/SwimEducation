Advanced Workouts

Challenging drills and high-intensity training for competitive swimmers

Training Techniques

Click on a drill title below to reveal details and learn how to perform each one effectively.

Endurance Sets
Swim 3x800m freestyle with minimal rest. Focus on steady breathing and pace to build endurance over distance.
Paddle Drills
Use hand paddles during 5x100m sets. Emphasize a strong, smooth pull to build power and improve stroke technique.
Kickboard Pyramid
Complete 25m, 50m, 75m, 100m kicks with a board, then work your way back down. Great for building leg strength.
Broken 200s
Swim 4x50m with 10 seconds rest between. Focus on holding consistent or descending splits for endurance pacing.
Sprint Intervals with Fins
Do 8x50m sprints using fins. Rest 20 seconds between reps. Builds explosive speed and enhances kick efficiency.

Tips for Advanced Swimmers

  • Use swim tools like paddles and snorkels to isolate and strengthen techniques.
  • Track split times to monitor pacing and performance improvements.
  • Incorporate dryland workouts for strength and flexibility.
  • Hydrate and stretch well before and after intense sets.
